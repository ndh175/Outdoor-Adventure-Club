President

  • Attending every meeting / speak at every meeting.
  • To coordinate with speakers / locations for presentations and events.
  • Direct the Vice President with the planning of weekly meetings.
  • To manage and oversee all other E-board members / assist with their activity planning.
  • To plan at least one Top Level outing / activity / trip per year for the club.
  • And most importantly, the president should give something unique back to the club to ensure its growth and to set a platform for future presidents and members.
  • Must attend CSA Training.

Last updated Jan 11, 2018

Vice President

  • The Vice President's job is to assist the president by helping out with club events and holding E-board members accountable for their duties.
  • Working with the President and Secretary to plan weekly meetings.
  • Direct oversight / point of contact for club members as well as E-Board.
  • Must plan at least one outing / activity / trip per year (Fall Trip).
  • Must attend CSA Training.

Last updated Jan 11, 2018

Treasurer

  • Maintain and keep track of membership cards and dues.
  • Collecting money from members.
  • Cash checks as well as maintain accounts with CSA.
  • Request funding and project / estimate spending for trips as well as events.
  • Making purchases for club through CSA.
  • Must plan at least one outing / activity / trip per semester.
  • Must attend CSA Training.

Last updated Jan 11, 2018

Secretary

  • Take notes at every meeting.
  • Fill out and turn in Equipment request forms through CSA.
  • E-mail club members updates / itineraries for trips, meetings, etc.
  • Assist other E-board members with tasks including:
    • Helping with the making of trip packets for club members.
    • Research on locations for trips.
    • Reserving sites for trips.
    • Keeping up to date on the policies and procedures that are to be followed at each site, or location the club travels to.
  • Update email list on Google Drive.
  • Assist the Vice President in the creation of itineraries for weekly meetings.
  • Help the Webmaster with posting a schedule of events and a summary of previous meetings on the website.
  • Must maintain Google Calendar by posting events and sending invites.
  • Must plan at least one outing / activity / trip per semester.
  • Must attend CSA Training.

Last updated Jan 11, 2018

Public Relations

  • Coordinate tabling at events to increase membership (Go for the Gold, Student Organization Fairs, etc).
  • Hang approved flyers created by the Creative Directors and Fundraising.
  • Maintain social media accounts by posting regularly and increasing followers by using the photos uploaded to Google Drive by the Creative Directors.
  • Pass on passwords / account info to next PR & SO member.
  • Must plan at least one outing / activity / trip per semester.

Last updated Jan 11, 2018

Safety Director

  • Must check trips for safety issues.
  • Handles Emergency Fund.
  • Makes sure liability waivers are signed and up to date.
  • Must plan at least one outing / activity / trip per semester.

Last updated Jan 11, 2018

Creative Director

  • Take photos / videos at major events & upload to Google Drive.
  • Work with the Fundraising and PR & SO to develop / create flyers for future events.
  • Coordinate with club members to collect photos / media.
  • Coordinate with PR & SO to upload photos to social media.
  • Coordinate with the Webmaster to upload photos / videos to the website.
  • Assist in the creation of any designs needed for club (membership cards, t-shirts, flyers, poster boards, etc).
  • Maintain our club website and GrizzOrgs.
  • Work alongside the Secretary to post upcoming events/a calendar and previous meeting summaries on the website and GrizzOrgs.
  • Must plan at least one outing / activity / trip per semester.

Last updated Jan 11, 2018

Fundraising

  • Must contact nearby stores and businesses in search of sponsors / donations.
  • Must organize fundraising tables / events.
  • Coordinate with Treasurer / other E-Board members to set up fundraising events / activities.
  • Must maintain relationship with business contacts as well as student body (flyers, etc.) Coordinate with Creative Directors to plan and publicize events.
  • Must plan at least one activity / outing / trip per semester.

Last updated Jan 11, 2018

Gear Manager

  • Must maintain inventory (Inventory History, etc).
  • Coordinate with Treasurer and other E-Board members to keep list of paying members updated.
  • Create and update a google form for members to sign out/ reserve gear whenever they would like.
  • Store & retrieve gear for members.
  • Check out gear to members.
  • Must plan at least one outing/ activity/ trip per semester.

Last updated Jan 11, 2018
